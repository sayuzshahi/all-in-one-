Namaste Handicraft is a treasure trove of exquisite handmade artifacts that celebrate the beauty of tradition and craftsmanship. Located in Nardevi, Kathmandu our shop offers a wide range of unique items, including intricate wood carvings, vibrant textiles, pottery, jewelry, and woven baskets, all created by skilled local artisans. Dedicated to ethical and sustainable practices, we support artisan communities and strive to preserve traditional craftsmanship. Whether you’re looking for a meaningful gift or a personal keepsake, our collection offers something special for everyone, with customization options available to suit your taste. Visit us at Nardevi, Kathmandu or explore our collection online at Instagram to bring home a piece of culture and artistry.





About Us

At Namaste Handicraft, we are passionate about preserving and promoting the rich heritage of handcrafted artistry. Our journey began with a vision to connect skilled artisans with those who value the beauty of handmade creations. Every item in our collection tells a story, reflecting the traditions, culture, and craftsmanship of its maker. We take pride in offering a curated selection of unique, high-quality handicrafts, from home décor and accessories to textiles and ceramics. Committed to ethical and sustainable practices, we work closely with local artisans, ensuring fair compensation and fostering a community that values creativity and cultural preservation. Whether you’re seeking a one-of-a-kind gift, a touch of tradition for your home, or a timeless keepsake, Namaste Handicraft is here to bring you closer to the art of handmade treasures. Visit us at Namaste Handicraft or online at Instagram to explore our collection and join us in celebrating the charm of handcrafted excellence.
